No Bake Chocolate Peanut Butter Oat Cookies

Description:

Chewy, delicious, and healthy no-bake chocolate chip oatmeal cookies with peanut butter.

These cookies are very easy to make, and don't require any baking!

Ingredients:

  • Peanut Butter
  • Coconut Oil
  • Oats
  • Chocolate Chips

Instructions:

  1. Whisk together the coconut oil, peanut butter, vanilla, and coconut sugar.
  2. Ditribute the mixture evenly onto a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
  3. Transfer to the freezer to set.
  4. Leave cookies in the freezer for 10 mintues.
