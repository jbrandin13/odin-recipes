Classic Chocolate Chip Cookies

Description:

Classic chocolate chip cookies, just like you remember from your childhood!

These cookies are delicious, with crisp edges and chewy middles.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup of softened butter
  • 1 cup of sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tsp of baking sode
  • 3 cups all purpose flour
  • 2 cups of semisweet chocolate chips

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees F.
  2. Mix the butter and sugger together in a bowl.
  3. Add eggs one at a time.
  4. Stir in the flour and chocolate chips.
  5. Spread dough onto a baking sheet, keeping cookies 2 inches apart.
  6. Bake in over about 10 minutes, until edges are browned.
