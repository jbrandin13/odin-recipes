Gluten Free Snickerdoodle Cookies

Description:

Perfect gluten-free snickerdoodle cookies.

Soft on the inside and crispy on the edges, with a great cinnamon taste!

Ingredients:

  • All purpose flour - 1.5 cups
  • Baking powder - 1 tsp
  • Salt - 1/4 tsp
  • Butter - 1/2 cup
  • Egg - 1
  • Cinnamon - 1.5 tsp

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F.
  2. Mix baking power, flour, and salt in a bowl.
  3. Whish butter and sugar (best using an electric mixer) until soft.
  4. Mix egg into the sugar and butter mixture, and slowly add flour mixture.
  5. Shape dough into balls.
  6. Roll dough in cinnamon and sugar mixture.
  7. Bak for 15 minutes and enjoy!
